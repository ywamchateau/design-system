Do

Warm cast, low-ISO grain, shallow depth of field. Faces, hands, thresholds — candid, unposed.

Natural light: kitchen windows, candlelight, overcast exteriors. Italic Cormorant captions over a bottom protection gradient.

Don't

No stock photography. No heavy retouching or skin smoothing. No cool/teal color grade.

No staged group shots with arms around shoulders. No lens flares, no HDR, no synthetic bokeh.
