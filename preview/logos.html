Do

Lead with the green mark on cream or paper. Use the icon alone only when the wordmark has appeared elsewhere on the surface.

Don't

Don't recolor the mark, add effects, place on busy photography without a tint, or stretch the lockup. Don't use the white mark on light backgrounds.
