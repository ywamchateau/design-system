# Slide templates

Five slide templates at 1280×720, echoing the Vision Document style from the Figma `/DELIVERABLES/PRESENTATION` frame.

- `TitleSlide.html` — full-bleed moss, Bona Title display, Korean line, small-caps meta at the bottom.
- `SectionSlide.html` — cream, large numbered section opener, moss hairline rule.
- `ContentSlide.html` — cream, four-item grid with moss left-border accents, bilingual item titles.
- `QuoteSlide.html` — ink, italic pull-quote with amber open-quote glyph.
- `ClosingSlide.html` — cream, two-column call-to-action with a stat card and primary CTA.

All share the footer pattern: `YWAM CHATEAUˆ` on the left, `NN / NN` page counter on the right, both in Bona Nova SC.
